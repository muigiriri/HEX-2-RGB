Convert HEX color (#)
to RGB color

Input HEX value:

 

RGB(red,green,blue)

Rules
1. The hash (#) at the begining is optional.
2. 7 characters or 4 characters (including the #).
3. 6 characters or 3 characters (excluding the #).
2. Valid letters a, b, c, d, e, f
4. Any number combinations E.g.(#143260)
5. Any letter combinations E.g.(#abfbcd)
6. Any numbers and letters combinations E.g.(#a23b9d)
