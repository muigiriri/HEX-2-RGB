Convert HEX color (#)
to RGB color

Type or paste HEX value:

 

RGB(red,green,blue)
#

Rules for the HEX value

1. 7 or 4 characters (with a '#' at the begining).
2. 6 or 3 characters (without the #).
3. E.g. #113300, 113300, #130 or 130
4. Any number combination E.g. #143260
5. Any letter combination E.g.(#abfbcd)
6. Valid letters a, b, c, d, e, f
7. Any numbers and letters combinations E.g.(#a23b9d)
